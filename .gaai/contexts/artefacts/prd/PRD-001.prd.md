# PRD-001: Callibrate MVP

## Purpose

Frame the full scope of the Callibrate MVP — a two-sided marketplace for AI integration experts and prospects — so that all Epics and Stories share a single coherent product vision.

---

## Problem Statement

**For AI integration experts:** They waste time on unqualified leads — wrong budget, no decision-maker, no timeline. Existing platforms charge commission on deals, creating legal complexity and no transparency. No platform delivers pre-qualified, pre-committed prospects with a booked call as the delivery unit.

**For companies seeking AI experts:** They struggle to identify relevant AI integration expertise efficiently. Generic freelance platforms offer no qualification, no transparent matching criteria, and no confidence that the expert has solved their specific problem before.

---

## Goals

- Deliver a working end-to-end marketplace loop: prospect qualifies → gets matched → books a call → expert gets billed (M1)
- Validate that both sides of the marketplace want the product before building (M0)
- Generate first revenue (M3: ≥10 calls payés, GMV ≥1 500€, ≥3 experts re-payants)
- Operate with zero manual intervention per transaction (solo founder constraint)

---

## Non-Goals

- Mobile native app
- Multi-language (FR first, EN post-MVP)
- Commission on deals (revenue = pay-per-call lead access fees only)
- Subscription plans (deferred to post-M3)
- Custom CRM integration beyond webhooks
- White-label version
- Dispute resolution workflow (manual at MVP)
- AI chatbot guidance during prospect form (quiz funnel is conversational, not chatbot)

---

## Users

**Experts (paying customers):**
AI integration consultants and freelancers. Want qualified, pre-screened prospects with confirmed budget, decision-maker access, and timeline. Pay per booked call only (no subscription, no upfront commitment).

**Prospects (product delivered to):**
Companies and business owners with a real AI automation need. Use the platform free. Come from niche satellite sites optimized per vertical (e-commerce, healthcare, finance, etc.).

---

## Key Requirements

### Platform architecture — 3-layer model
Each layer consumes the layer below it exclusively.

- **Layer 1 — Data:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) — all structured data, no business logic
- **Layer 2 — Services/API:** Cloudflare Workers (API, matching engine, AI extraction, score computation) + Queues + n8n — domain-agnostic, no UI concerns
- **Layer 3 — Interfaces:** Two tracks, each can host N platforms consuming Layer 2 only:
  - *Track 3.1 — Expert UIs:* callibrate.io (registration, profile, dashboard, billing)
  - *Track 3.2 — Prospect UIs:* callibrate.ai (directory + engine funnel) · satellite sites (N niche inbound funnels, embedded booking widget, data centralized in Layer 2)

### Expert side
- Expert can register, build a profile with matching criteria (JSONB-based, flexible)
- Expert connects Google Calendar via Cal.com managed users
- Expert receives lead notifications on booking
- Expert pays via Lemon Squeezy (Merchant of Record — international tax compliance)

### Prospect side
- Prospect completes a conversational quiz funnel (5–7 questions, progressive disclosure)
- Quiz schema is configurable per satellite (satellite_configs.quiz_schema)
- Prospect sees anonymized match results (top-3 experts) before email capture
- Full profiles + booking links unlocked after email capture

### Matching engine
- Bi-directional: expert.profile vs prospect.requirements + expert.preferences vs prospect attributes
- JSONB-based flexible criteria (configurable weights per satellite via matching_weights)
- Score 0–100 with per-criterion breakdown (skills overlap, industry, budget, timeline, language)
- Deal-breaker logic: expert preferences can produce score = 0

### Booking & billing
- Cal.com managed users: 20-min intro call, Google Meet auto-generated
- Booking confirmed → lead-billing queue → Lemon Squeezy one-time checkout (pay-per-call)
- Reminders sent automatically via Resend

### AI freetext extraction
- Prospect describes project in natural language (freetext, max 2000 chars)
- Claude (claude-haiku-4-5) extracts structured `ProspectRequirements` JSONB with per-field confidence scores
- Low-confidence fields (< 0.7) trigger max 2–3 targeted confirmation questions
- High-confidence fields skip confirmation entirely — ready to match immediately
- Extraction endpoint: `POST /api/extract` (E06S08)

### Composite score & trust system
- `composite_score = call_experience_avg × 0.35 + trust_score × 0.20 + client_satisfaction_avg × 0.20 + hire_rate × 0.10 + recency_score × 0.15`
- Used as tiebreaker in match ranking; displayed to prospects as anonymized quality tier (New / Rising / Established / Top)
- Computed automatically by score worker (E06S09) after each feedback event

### Tri-directional feedback loop
- Prospect rates call experience J+7 (1–5 scale)
- Prospect rates project satisfaction J+45 (1–10 scale)
- Expert evaluates lead quality J+48 (1–10 scale) + optional conversion declaration
- n8n orchestrates time-based survey triggers; all signals feed composite_score computation

### Infrastructure
- Edge runtime: Cloudflare Workers (not Pages)
- DB + Auth: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Supabase Auth)
- Async: Cloudflare Queues + Workflows
- Business automation: n8n
- Email: Resend
