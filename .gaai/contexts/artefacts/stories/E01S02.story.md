# Story E01S02: Expert-Side Hand Raiser Campaign

## User Story

**As a** founder validating expert-side demand,
**I want** AI integration experts to publicly raise their hand in response to a targeted post,
**so that** I have unbiased evidence that experts will pay for qualified leads before building anything.

---

## Context

The expert side is the paying customer. Validating their willingness to engage (and implicitly pay) before building is the highest-priority risk to eliminate. A public Hand Raiser post on LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and Facebook Groups generates honest signal — people either respond or they don't, with no social pressure to be polite.

Promise template (adapt language from E01S01 findings):
> "Si je pouvais vous envoyer 3 à 5 leads qualifiés par mois — budget confirmé, décisionnaire identifié, projet d'automatisation IA défini — est-ce que ça vous intéresserait ? Commentez 'moi' ci-dessous."

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AC1: 50–100 AI integration experts/consultants added as connections on LinkedIn before posting
- [ ] AC2: Hand Raiser post published on LinkedIn using promise language from E01S01
- [ ] AC3: Same post (adapted) published on X/Twitter
- [ ] AC4: Same post (adapted) published in at least 2 relevant Facebook Groups (entrepreneur / B2B / AI)
- [ ] AC5: All commenters logged (name, profile, platform) within 48h of each post going live
- [ ] AC6: Gate assessed: ≥10 qualified experts raise their hand across all platforms combined

---

## Out of Scope

- Responding to hand raisers with a sales pitch or video (not at this stage)
- Paid promotion of posts
- DM outreach before posting

---

## Dependencies

- E01S01 (promise language must be sharpened before posting)

---

## Notes

"Qualified expert" = profile shows they are an active AI consultant, integrator, or automation freelance. Not a prospect, not a student. Gate is 10 across all platforms combined — not per platform.

If gate fails (< 10 raisers): rewrite the promise and repost before moving to E01S03.
