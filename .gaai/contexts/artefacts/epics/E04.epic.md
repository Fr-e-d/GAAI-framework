# Epic E04: Qualified Call Gets Booked and Confirmed

## Purpose

After a prospect selects an expert, a qualified call is booked via an embedded Cal.com widget and both parties see a confirmation — so the platform delivers its core monetisable unit (the booked call) with zero manual intervention.

> **Layer constraint:** This epic delivers Layer 3 UI only. The Cal.com webhook handling, email sending, reminder logic, lead recording, and billing trigger are all Layer 2 — already built in E06S04 and E06S06. E04 consumes those results and renders the UX.

---

## Scope

- Cal.com booking widget embedded on callibrate.ai and satellite sites (prospect-side — Layer 3.2)
- Booking confirmation page: displays meeting link, date/time, expert details (prospect-side — Layer 3.2)
- Expert lead page on callibrate.io: shows new lead with prospect context summary (expert-side — Layer 3.1)

---

## Out of Scope

- Email confirmation sending (Layer 2 — E06S06 AC1)
- Automated reminders (Layer 2 — n8n, triggered by E06S06 AC10)
- Lead delivery notification email to expert (Layer 2 — E06S06 AC1/AC7)
- Booked call recorded as a lead (Layer 2 — E06S04 webhook + E06S06 lead-billing)
- Video call hosting (prospect and expert use Google Meet / Teams — generated by Cal.com)
- Post-call feedback collection UI (E05S05)
- Lead quality dispute resolution (post-MVP)

---

## Stories

- E04S01: Prospect can book a call slot via the Cal.com booking widget (embedded on satellite or callibrate.ai)
- E04S02: Prospect sees a booking confirmation page with meeting link, date/time, and expert summary
- E04S03: Expert sees a new lead card on their callibrate.io dashboard with prospect context summary

---

## Success Metrics

- ≥35% of prospect-expert selections result in a confirmed booked call
- Zero manual interventions required per booking
- Lead→booked call time <72 hours
- Widget load-to-booking completion rate ≥70%

---

## Dependencies

- E03 (Prospect must be matched before booking is possible)
- E02 (Expert Cal.com calendar must be connected, dashboard must exist)
- E06 (E06S04 Cal.com webhook + E06S06 email/lead workers must be active)
