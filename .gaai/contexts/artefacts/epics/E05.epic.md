# Epic E05: Expert Pays for Lead Access and Tracks ROI

## Purpose

An expert is billed automatically when a prospect books a call (pay-per-call), can evaluate the lead quality after the call, declare conversion, and track their full pipeline and ROI — so the platform generates revenue, improves over time, and experts can measure the value they receive.

> **Layer constraint:** This epic delivers Layer 3.1 UI only (callibrate.io). The billing trigger, score computation, and survey scheduling are Layer 2 — already built in E06S06 and E06S09. E05 consumes those APIs and renders the expert-facing dashboard and forms.

---

## Scope

- **Pay-per-call billing:** 100–200€ per booked call — triggered automatically on Cal.com booking confirmation
- **Lemon Squeezy checkout:** Expert receives a checkout link and completes payment (one-time, not recurring) — Lemon Squeezy acts as Merchant of Record (international tax handled)
- **Expert lead dashboard:** Leads received, calls booked, conversion tracking, composite score visibility
- **Lead evaluation (J+48):** Expert scores lead quality (1–10) and optionally declares conversion — feeds the matching engine feedback loop
- **Conversion declaration:** Expert voluntarily reports if the project started/closed — contributes to `hire_rate` component of composite score
- **Premium options (post-MVP):** Visibility boost, Top Match badge

---

## Out of Scope

- Subscription plans (deferred to post-M3)
- Invoice generation beyond Lemon Squeezy receipts (LS generates receipts automatically)
- Multi-currency (EUR only at MVP)
- Refund/dispute workflow (manual at MVP)
- Prospect-side payment (prospects use free)
- Automatic stored-payment-method billing (post-MVP — expert must click through checkout at MVP)

---

## Stories

- E05S01: Expert reviews pending call billing and initiates Lemon Squeezy checkout
- E05S02: Expert completes payment via Lemon Squeezy checkout and lead status updates to `billed`
- E05S03: Expert receives a lead notification (email + dashboard) when a call is booked and paid
- E05S04: Expert can view their lead pipeline and conversion metrics on the dashboard
- E05S05: Expert evaluates lead quality (J+48) and optionally declares conversion

---

## Success Metrics

- ≥10 calls payés by Day 90
- GMV ≥ 1 500€ by Day 90
- ≥3 experts having paid for ≥2 leads (re-purchase signal)
- Lead evaluation rate ≥ 60% (experts submitting E05S05 within J+72)
- Expert satisfaction with lead quality ≥ 7/10 (from lead evaluations)

---

## Dependencies

- E04 (booked calls must exist before lead delivery can be monetised)
- E06 (lead-billing queue, Lemon Squeezy integration, and score computation worker must be active)
